Lasagna

How to make a steaming hot lasagna

Today we're going to learn how to make a memorable lasagna. This lasagna will blow you away on all levels. Guaranteed.

If You cannot find any ingredients for our recipe, please don't make this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzarella Cheese and Ricotta Cheese/li>

Steps

  1. Warm Oven, Warm Pan, Boil Pasta Water
  2. Brown Beef & Drain, Boil Pasta Noodles
  3. Layer Ricotta, Tomato Sauce, Beef and Pasta Noodles
  4. Cover with Mozzarella Cheese and Foil
  5. Bake at 400 F for 80 Minutes
