Chicken Breast

How to make a Juicy Chicken Breast

Today we're going to learn cook a perfectly juicy Chicken Breast. We recommend Pan Searing and then Baking for this recipe.

Season to your desired

Ingredients

  • Raw Chicken Breast
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Heat Pan and Oven to select Temperature
  2. Pour Teaspoon Oil after Pan is Heated
  3. Sear Breast for 4 Minutes, Then Flip for 4 more Minutes
  4. Bake for an additional 8-10 minutes
  5. Optional Toss in barbeque sauce
